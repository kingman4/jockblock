Returns & Refunds

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If Jock Block doesn't meet your expectations, we'll make it right.

Our 30-Day Return Policy

You may return your Jock Block purchase within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the product price. This applies to both opened and unopened products.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return:

  • The return request must be made within 30 days of delivery
  • You must have proof of purchase (order confirmation email)
  • The product must not have been misused or tampered with

How to Request a Return

To initiate a return, please contact us with:

  • Your order number or confirmation email
  • The reason for your return
  • Whether you'd like a refund or replacement

Use our contact form to submit your return request.

Refund Process

Once we receive your return request:

  • We'll review your request within 1-2 business days
  • If approved, we'll provide return shipping instructions (if applicable)
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval
  • The refund will be credited to your original payment method

Shipping Costs

  • Defective or damaged products: We cover return shipping costs
  • Change of mind: Customer is responsible for return shipping
  • Original shipping fees: Non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Exchanges

We currently only offer one product, so exchanges are not applicable. If you'd like to purchase additional units, please place a new order after your refund is processed.

Damaged or Defective Products

If your product arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll send a replacement at no additional cost or provide a full refund, including shipping.
